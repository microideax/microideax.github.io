Yao Chen

I am a Research Scientist with Advanced Digital Sciences Center (ADSC), a research center of University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) based in Singapore. Currently, I am leading the Hardware and Data Analytics group of ADSC on quite some attractive research topics under the supervision of Prof. Deming Chen and Prof. Marianne Winslett.

I have obtained my Bachelor and PhD from Nankai University in 2010 and 2016, both majored in Electronic Science and Technology. I have also spent two years (2013.9-2015.7) in the CSL lab in the UIUC as a visiting scholar.

Research Interests

I'm interested in FPGA enabled High Performance Computing, Edge Computing, High Level Synthesis, Distributed Systems. Some of my work also related to Internet of Things, Bioinformatics and High Precision/Accuracy Analog ASIC Design. For the details, please refer to my Projects and Publications.
