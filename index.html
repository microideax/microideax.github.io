Biography

I am a Professor with the School of Computer Science and Technology at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). My work explores efficient computer systems through specialized hardware, software–hardware co-design, and automation.

Before joining HUST, I was a Research Assistant Professor at the National University of Singapore, where I collaborated with Prof. Bingsheng He and Prof. Weng-Fai Wong. Earlier, I was a Senior Research Scientist at the Advanced Digital Sciences Center, a UIUC-affiliated research center in Singapore.

我任职于华中科技大学计算机科学与技术学院，担任教授。我的研究聚焦于专用硬件、软硬件协同设计和设计自动化，以构建高效的计算机系统。

加入华中科技大学之前，我曾任新加坡国立大学计算机学院研究助理教授，与何丙胜教授和黄荣辉教授合作开展多项研究。此前，我曾任伊利诺伊大学厄巴纳–香槟分校设在新加坡的高级数字科学研究中心高级研究科学家。
