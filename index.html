Yao Chen

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Advanced Digital Sciences Center (ADSC), a research center of University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) based in Singapore.

I did my Bachelor and PhD at Nankai University from 2010 to 2016, majored in Electronic Science and Technology. I have also spent two years (2013-2015) in the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in the CSL lab as a visiting PhD student.
