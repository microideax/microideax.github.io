Yao Chen

I am a Postdoc researcher at Advanced Digital Sciences Center (ADSC), a research center of University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) based in Singapore.

I did my Bachelor and PhD at Nankai University from 2006 to 2016, both majored in Electronic Science and Technology. I have also spent two years (2013-2015) in UIUC in the CSL lab as a visiting scholar.

Email  /  CV  /  Google Scholar  /  LinkedIn

Research Interests

I'm interested in FPGA enabled High Performance Computing, High Level Synthesis, Distributed Systems. Some of my work also related to Internet of Things, Bioinformatics and High Precision/Accuracy Analog ASIC Design. For the details, please refer to my Projects and Publications.
